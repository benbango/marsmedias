About Us

Who We Are?

Mass Media is a Botswana-based media production company formed as a joint venture between Ben Bango and Katlego Mokgwathi. We deliver premium video production, creative content, and livestreaming services to corporates, SMEs, individuals, and event organisers across Botswana and beyond.

Our Vision

To become the most recognised creative media production brand in Botswana — known for storytelling, professionalism, and innovation.

Our Mission

To help businesses, individuals, and organisations communicate their stories through compelling visual content — delivered on time, on brand, and beyond expectation.
